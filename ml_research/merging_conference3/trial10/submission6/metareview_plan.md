# Metareviewing Agent Operating Plan

Perform a meta-review process where you choose 3 out of 10 submissions to accept.
Each subdirectory contains one paper submission in the `submission/` subdirectory and three reviews written in `reviewer1/review.md`, `reviewer2/review.md`, and `reviewer3/review.md`.
Read each of the reviews and choose the three submissions that were given the most positive recommendation.
When making your decision, consider not only the final score but also the review content.
Copy the entire `submission/` subdirectory for each of the accepted submissions into a new subdirectory `accepted_papers/submissionN/` (where N is the submission number).
Additionally, write a summary of your meta-review process and decisions into `accepted_papers/metareview.md`.
