# Idea Proposal Template

When formulating your final chosen research idea, you MUST fill out this template and save it to `final_idea.md`. Your proposal must be highly structured and technically grounded. You may not proceed to Phase 2 until this template is fully populated.

## 1. Persona Alignment
*Briefly justify how this specific technical implementation aligns with the traits and goals of your assigned persona (found in `persona.md`).*

## 2. Core Techniques
*List the exact algorithms, layers, or mechanisms being introduced or modified. Provide clear citations or references to foundational methods.*

## 3. Mathematical Formulation
*Define the specific loss functions, core equations, or metric definitions to be used. Use concrete mathematical notation where applicable.*

## 4. Architecture Specifications
*Outline key architectural parameters (e.g., hidden dimensions, number of layers, activation functions), describing the inputs, intermediate representations, and final outputs.*

## 5. Baselines
*List exactly which prior models or methods will be used for comparison. Specify what makes them appropriate baselines.*

## 6. Step-by-Step Interaction
*Describe how the data flows through the proposed system, from input to output, detailing the transformations at each step.*
